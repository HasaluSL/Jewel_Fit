This project mainly focuses on applying it to jewelry shops.
A common problem that is faced by every jewelry shop owner is shoplifting.
At an average most of these shoplifters do their work during fit-on and checking out items. Due to the high cost of these jewelry losing even one necklace can be a huge profit loss.
Our programming and setup can open a gateway to the solution.
Customers can use an indirect way of checking out their products creating a customer satisfaction while the shop owners can optimize their security and profit making it a win-win situation.  

Technology

Face Filtering technology is not new and it has been used alot.
It is not used for the right purpose since we only see it in entertainment in the form of Instagram Filters or SnapChat Filters.

How does it work?

It works by detecting your or anyone's face.
This is a real time face tracking mechanism at work.
After the preferred necklace i.e is selected it will appear on the screen with a optional slider to adjust it to the users face.
